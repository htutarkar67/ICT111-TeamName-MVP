# Problem Notes

## Selected Case

Movie Recommendation System

## Target Respondents

| Respondent Type          | Why This Respondent Matters                         | Planned Number | Actual Number |
| ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------- | -------------: | ------------: |
| University Students      | Frequently watch movies and use streaming platforms |             12 |            12 |
| Movie Enthusiasts        | Regularly search for and explore new movies         |              5 |             5 |
| Streaming Platform Users | Experience challenges finding suitable content      |              5 |             5 |

## Observed / Reported Problem

Many respondents reported that choosing a movie is difficult because streaming platforms offer thousands of options. Users often spend a long time browsing before selecting a movie and sometimes end up watching content that does not match their interests. Many users also find it difficult to discover new movies based on their preferences.

## Current Alternatives or Workarounds

| Workaround                            | Who Uses It?                | Weakness / Pain                               | Evidence Source      |
| ------------------------------------- | --------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Browsing streaming platforms manually | Students, streaming users   | Time-consuming and overwhelming               | Multiple respondents |
| Asking friends for recommendations    | Students, movie enthusiasts | Recommendations may not match personal tastes | Multiple respondents |
| Searching Google reviews              | All users                   | Requires extra effort and research            | Multiple respondents |
| Watching trending movies              | Streaming users             | Popular movies may not fit user preferences   | Multiple respondents |
| Social media recommendations          | All users                   | Information can be unreliable or biased       | Multiple respondents |

## Repeated Pain Points

| Pain Point                        | Number of Mentions | Example Evidence                             | Severity    |
| --------------------------------- | -----------------: | -------------------------------------------- | ----------- |
| Too many movie choices            |                 11 | Users feel overwhelmed by available options  | High        |
| Time spent searching              |                 10 | Users browse for a long time before choosing | High        |
| Recommendations not personalized  |                  8 | Suggested movies do not match interests      | High        |
| Difficulty discovering new movies |                  7 | Users repeatedly watch similar content       | Medium-High |
| Information overload              |                  5 | Too many reviews and ratings to compare      | Medium      |

## Consequences

| Consequence                  | Example                                         | Evidence Strength |
| ---------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| Wasted time                  | Users spend excessive time searching for movies | Strong            |
| Poor viewing experience      | Users choose movies they do not enjoy           | Strong            |
| Missed content opportunities | Users fail to discover suitable movies          | Strong            |
| Frustration                  | Difficulty selecting content reduces enjoyment  | Strong            |
| Repetitive viewing habits    | Users repeatedly watch familiar movies          | Moderate          |

## Notes for MVP Direction

* Create a personalized movie recommendation platform.
* Allow users to select favorite genres and preferences.
* Recommend movies based on user interests.
* Include movie search and filtering features.
* Display movie ratings, genres, and descriptions.
* Allow users to save favorite movies to a watchlist.
* Provide recommendations for similar movies.
* Keep the interface simple and user-friendly.
* Ensure the platform works on desktop and mobile devices.
* Focus on helping users discover relevant movies quickly.
